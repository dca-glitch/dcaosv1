# AI Modules

## Purpose

AI modules are planned future modules for DCA OS v1.

They should be added only after the platform foundation is stable.

## Planned AI Areas

Potential AI modules:

- AI task assistant
- AI content planner
- AI SEO research
- AI draft generator
- AI report generator
- AI client dashboard insights
- AI finance assistant
- AI CRM assistant
- AI workflow automation
- AI knowledge base
- AI module builder assistant

## AI Design Rule

AI features should support human-reviewed workflows.

AI should help prepare, summarize, draft, classify, and recommend.

Critical business actions should remain reviewable.

## Integration Direction

AI modules should integrate with:

- module registry
- permissions
- tenant context
- audit logs
- dashboards
- reports

## Future AI Workflow Pattern

A future AI workflow may include:

1. input source
2. context collection
3. AI processing
4. draft output
5. human review
6. approval
7. publication or execution
8. audit record

## Out of Scope Now

No AI runtime provider integration is active in the current foundation.
